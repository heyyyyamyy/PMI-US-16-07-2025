The Benefits of Volunteering

Volunteering is a great way to meet enthusiastic fellow project professionals and give something back to to the project management profession. Experience volunteering for yourself by joining our team of PMI UK volunteers. In the video on the left, our previous Director of Volunteering Nigel Smith introduces the benefits of volunteering with the chapter.

Below are the current volunteering opportunities available within the PMI UK Chapter. Click on the title bar of each opportunity for more information.

Build a Professional Network

Enhance the profession of project management, reap the benefits of expanding connections, and leverage career advancement opportunities all while volunteering with PMI.

Gain Skills and Experiences

Develop and sharpen vital new skills, including leadership, collaboration and team-building techniques.

Develop as a Leader

Leverage opportunities to develop your personal leadership skills while making a difference in your profession, community, and life.
