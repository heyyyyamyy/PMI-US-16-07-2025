Certified Scrum Master (CSM)®

Entry-level to advanced Agile project management roles

The Scrum Master Certification is globally recognized for individuals facilitating Agile teams and Scrum framework adoption. It demonstrates your ability to help teams work efficiently by following Agile values and Scrum principles.

Scrum Master certification equips professionals with the skills to support product owners, manage team dynamics, and remove obstacles that hinder productivity in a fast-paced Agile environment.

Is Scrum Master Right for You?

Eligibility Requirements

Set A (CSM)

  • Open to all educational backgrounds
  • Completion of a 2-day Certified Scrum Master® training course
  • Pass the Scrum Alliance CSM exam

Set B (Advanced CSM)

  • Active Certified Scrum Master® certification
  • At least 12 months of Scrum Master experience within the past 5 years
  • Completion of an Advanced Certified Scrum Master (A-CSM®) training

Set C (Certified Scrum Professional)

  • Active Advanced Certified Scrum Master® certification
  • At least 24 months of Scrum Master experience within the past 5 years
  • Completion of Certified Scrum Professional® training and successful evaluation

Maintaining your Scrum Master Certification

To maintain your Scrum certification, you must earn Scrum Education Units (SEUs) through learning, teaching, and community involvement.

  • CSM requires renewal every 2 years with 20 SEUs
  • A-CSM requires 30 SEUs
  • CSP-SM requires 40 SEUs
  • Engage in continuous learning, coaching, and knowledge sharing

Maintaining certification demonstrates your continuous engagement in Agile practices and keeps you aligned with industry evolution.
