Careers at PMI

As a PMI employee, you will have the opportunity to interact with colleagues and contribute to projects across the globe.

You will help Project Professionals, teams and enterprises by equipping them with the tools, trainings and methodologies they need to maximize project success.

We value and nurture an environment of inclusivity and diversity, and a friendly culture of communication and collaboration.

We do not discriminate on the basis of race, sex, gender, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, marital or familial status, age, ancestry, disability, genetic information or any other characteristic protected by applicable law. PMI is an equal opportunity employer, and we welcome any qualified individual to apply.

In addition to offering opportunities to hone your skills and enhance career development, PMI provides an excellent total package, with compensation and benefits based upon your geographic location.

  • Competitive compensation
  • Award opportunities
  • Generous benefits and paid time off
  • Flexible work options
  • Skill development opportunities
